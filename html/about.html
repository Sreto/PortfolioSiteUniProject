I am 23 years old, and I am a recent information technology graduate from Technical College Čačak, Serbia. Since I was a kid, computers, video games, animation and drawing have been my passion. As I was curious and eager for new things, the endless video game worlds and amazing adventures of my favourite animated characters were more and more attracting me and I have always felt like i was part of them. It was difficult to choose just one field, so i decided to combine all of them.

I want to become someone who will create new worlds and give life to fictional characters. The path to this goal is a long one, but with enough persistence and willpower I hope to create something that everyone will love!

As one wise man said: "Around here, however, we don’t look backwards for very long. We keep moving forward, opening up new doors and doing new things, because we’re curious… and curiosity keeps leading us down new paths."
